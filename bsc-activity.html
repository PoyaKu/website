Important
For users who make a deposit over $100 USD or more at BSC dForce Yield Markets ( https://markets.dforce.network/bsc ) and holds the deposit for over 72 hours will be entered into a giveaway. The winner will be determined at an increment of 30 transactions, (i.e. the 30th, 60th, 90th, 120th…onwards). For example, the 30th depositor, the 60th depositor…and onwards would each win $50 USD worth of DF. There will be a maximum of 30 total winners.
We will randomly pick 20 users who make transactions at BSC dForce Trade ( https://trade.dforce.network ) to give away $50 USD worth of DF each. In addition, we will randomly pick 5 winners from users whose transaction hash includes DF (example: 0x38jsfhiw9fh34df0ex…) to give away $100 USD worth of DF each.
For users who participate in our Christmas activity via the following 3 wallets that support Binance Smart Chain (TokenPocket、BitKeep、Mathwallet), we will randomly pick 10 addresses out of each wallet to give away $50 USD worth of DF each.
